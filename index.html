Is the Bronx the Most Dangerous New York City Borough to Live in in 2025?

New data reveals patterns of gun violence across the city's northernmost borough.

By Maddie Ritter
Nov. 6, 2025

The City of New York released a dataset to data.gov showing locations of where shootings have occurred in different boroughs, providing a detailed look at gun violence patterns across the five boroughs.

The data shows various location types where shooting incidents occurred throughout the Bronx in 2025, with housing areas and public streets showing the highest concentration of incidents.
